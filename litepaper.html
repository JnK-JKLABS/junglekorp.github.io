JungleKorp Litepaper

Introduction

JungleKorp is a Web3 ecosystem built around the $BANANAJ token and a community of NFT holders, offering real utilities, regular airdrops, and a gamified economy. It draws strength from NFT culture and a unique jungle universe.

Goals

  • Build a strong, scalable, and transparent Web3 environment.
  • Reward early adopters via staking, airdrops, and NFT utility.
  • Launch a native DEX (JKSwap) for trading ecosystem tokens.

  • Blockchain: ApeChain
  • Decimals: 18
